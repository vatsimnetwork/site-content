The Virtual Air Traffic Simulation Network (VATSIM) is an organization, which provides flight simulation and air traffic control enthusiasts with a network of computers to which they can log on via the Internet and engage in flight and air traffic simulation. This agreement covers the usage of the Virtual Air Traffic Simulation Network (hereinafter referred to as "VATSIM"). Individuals making an application to join VATSIM and those logging on to any of the network of computers joined together to comprise the physical VATSIM network as well as those additional computers defined in Article VI of the VATSIM Code of Regulations and otherwise utilized by VATSIM to provide services are hereinafter referred to as "Users" and/or "Members". The services are offered to you, the User, by VATSIM, conditioned on your acceptance, without modification, of the terms, conditions and notices contained herein. By submitting an application and being accepted as a member of VATSIM, you hereby agree to all of the terms, conditions and notices contained in this User Agreement, the VATSIM Code of Regulations and the VATSIM Code of Conduct.

The amendment history to this User Agreement may be found in Appendix "C" of the VATSIM.net Code of Regulations.


## 1. Use of VATSIM Services
* **1.1** While using VATSIM Services, you are permitted to engage in online flight and air traffic control simulation. You may also participate in forums, mailing lists, chats, and download software, data and other information made available by VATSIM and its affiliates. In addition, you agree to provide usage data which may be used by VATSIM and its affiliates.
Your activity in forums, mailing lists and chats is subject to review, modification and deletion, without notice, by authorized VATSIM personnel. Files downloaded may be subject to posted limitations on usage, reproduction and/or dissemination and you  are responsible for adhering to such limitations in exchange for the use of such software. The use of copyrighted VATSIM property which includes, but is not limited to, names and logos is restricted and may not be used by anyone without the express written consent of VATSIM. In addition, the use of copyrighted VATSIM data and statistics such as online time is reserved for the use of VATSIM, its members and affiliated organizations and groups. Redistribution or any other use of such data is prohibited without the express written consent of VATSIM.
* **1.2** To use VATSIM services, you must first register as a member of VATSIM at http://vatsim.net. Only registered members in good standing may fly online as a pilot and provide ATC as an air traffic controller.
* **1.3** During the registration process you must use your full first and last names. Each time you log onto the VATSIM.net network through the use of any approved pilot or controller software client(s), you must use a name format permitted by the VATSIM Code of Conduct.
* **1.4** You may register for and hold only one VATSIM registration and you may hold only one VATSIM identification number and password. This identification number and password are issued only to you and are neither transferable nor may they be used by any other person. The VATSIM identification number(s) issued to you remains at all times the property of VATSIM.net and may be revoked at any time pursuant to the terms of this Agreement, the VATSIM Code of Regulations and the VATSIM Code of Conduct.
* **1.5** By submitting an application, being accepted as a member of VATSIM and by logging onto the VATSIM.net network, a member agrees to be bound by the terms of this User Agreement, the VATSIM Code of Regulations and the VATSIM Code of Conduct.
* **1.6** The owners of VATSIM.net reserve the right to terminate a Member's account and/or network access privileges at any time without notice or limitation.

## 2. Connecting to the VATSIM Network
* **2.1** Whilst logging onto VATSIM, you must use a name format permitted by the VATSIM Code of Conduct. Failure to comply could lead to a suspension of your membership privileges and possible termination of your membership account.
* **2.2** VATSIM is a network of privately owned server computers that are connected via the Internet. Only software approved by the Board of Governors of VATSIM.net and listed as approved on the VATSIM.net website may be used to connect to, send data to or receive data from any server within the VATSIM.net network. For the purposes of this paragraph, the phrase 'send data' shall include passing data to another program acting as a proxy agent such that the data is then sent to the network, as well as sending the data directly to the network. This paragraph shall cover communications with the data and statistics servers, the certificates server and all other client servers which comprise the VATSIM network. Connecting or attempting to connect with non-approved software is prohibited and could lead to a suspension of your membership privileges and possible termination of your membership account.
* **2.3** Except as provided for by this section, a member is permitted to make only one connection at any one time under his or her account number (CID) to the VATSIM.net network. Multiple connections either as a pilot, controller or a combination of the two are not permitted. However, if a controller is logged on to the VATSIM.net network as a facility which is permitted to provide a voice Automatic Terminal Information Service (ATIS) as provided for by Paragraph C(11) of the Code of Conduct, then such controller may create up to two connections using his or her CID for purposes of providing ATIS to pilots. The first connection shall be used as a 'Controller' connection and the second connection shall be used exclusively as an 'ATIS' connection. Under no circumstances may such a controller create more than two connections.
